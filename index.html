About us

Gideon Restaurant is a casual dining, Kenyan restaurant founded in 2021. The first restaurant was first opened at Highrise which later closed down allowing expansion into 3 other branches in Nairobi (1) and Kiambu (2)

Provide home recipes made with love, made fresh and locally sourced to create a thriving ecosystem of suppliers and employees. Our values of passion, quality, integrity and dignity creates an environment that allows all walks of life to flourish.
